Find Events at a Campus Near You

Intervarsity Christian Fellowship helps inspire youth students and graduates to follow Jesus. You can find programs at High schools and Universities near you. The Intervarsity program is available at many different university campuses, where you can find Christ and be with people to take part in community events such as potlucks, game nights and scavenger hunts.

About Us

InterVarsity Christian Fellowship is a community of students at Carleton University. We are a place for people to experience God's love, explore the Christian faith, and/or grow in their relationship with Jesus while at university. The things we do together include studying the Bible, learning how to talk with God as individuals and collectively, supporting each other as peers and as mentors, and enjoying the good things of life together.
