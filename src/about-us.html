About Us

We're a group of university students of all backgrounds who want to learn more about Jesus, live out His teachings together, and learn what it means to be His followers. We learn from the Christian Bible together in weekly discussions, we pray together, and we support each other in the highs and lows of university life. We are a safe space for people to explore faith, ask their questions, and gather the information necessary to decide whether or not they want to follow Jesus, too, free of pressure and with the support and love we can offer. We also offer a stable community of friendship and home-away-from-home for anyone who travelled to Ottawa for their studies and needs a place to land.

Our Mission

We have two main goals that we would like to achieve. Firstly, we would like to spread the gospel to the students around the campus. Secondly, we would like to be a place where those that are hurting, or need help, or feel lost can feel comforted and be part of a group.

The gospel is important because that is where Christans obtain most of their knowledge and understanding of Jesus. The teachings of Jesus can help non-belivers understand what it is truly like to be a Christan and to lead a Christan life. Ultimately, people are converted through the Holy Spirit. Spreading the gospel is a way for us to work with the Holy Spirit so that people can find their faith.

People often feel alone in this world because they find that there is no meaning to life. In joining Intervarsity, you can find what it is like to be a Christan and to have purpose. You will also be able to partake in events with others who are in the club, and make friends with people you would have otherwise not known. At Intervarsity, people are able to pray for you, helping you overcome the obstacles that weight you down.
