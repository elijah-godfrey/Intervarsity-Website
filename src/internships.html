Internships

Campus Internships

Campus internships allow students to apply their learning at school in a professional environment. Students may develop and learn skills from professionals to be able to gain ministry experience and to help determine whether a position in the ministry is a good fit for them.


High School Internships

Highschool internships are for those who want to take their ministry experience to the next step by going into a different environment than what they are used to. You will be walking alongside students through their journey in highschool, guiding them on a path that can set them up for the future. This is typically for 3rd and 4th year students who are wanting to develop their ministry skills and find whether teaching a younger generation is the right fit for them.

Camp Internships

Over the summer, students can participate in monthly long camp internships where they will be able to minister with friends to help spread the gospel to the younger generation. There is also the option to take part in the L.E.A.D program which will span from September to June. Students will get the opportunity to learn alongside those that are more experienced, helping them get a sense of ministry and develop leadership roles.


InterVarsity Christian Fellowship is a community of students at Carleton University. We are a place for people to experience God's love, explore the Christian faith, and grow in our relationship with Jesus while at university.
