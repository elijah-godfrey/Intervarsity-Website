Work With Us

Job Opportunities

Intervarsity Undegraduate Ministry is found on college and university campuses. Our primary focus is to help undergruate students who are struggling with their faith in Christ. We are reminded in Philippians chapter 2 verse 15 to 16, where God tells us to be like beacons of light, holding out to others the Word of Life. Our vision is to see students and faculty transformed, campuses renewed, and to renew strength to those that have lost their way.

Intervarsity is looking for students that can help organize events and activites at their university and college campuses. It is ideal to have interpersonal skills and to be able to spread the gospel to your peers and fellow students around you. Student leaders help organize events such as bible study, prayer pods, scavenger hunts and much more.

Intervarsity Press serves as a form of media that allows others to be educated and informed on Christianity. Publish resources that encourage people to walk with Jesus. If you have any questions or would like to apply, click here.

Intervarsity provides volunteering opportunities for those that would like to help others grow in their relationship with Christ. Hearing the Gospel can encourage and strengthen their walk with Christ. When you serve and devote your time towards helping other people you can sense a feeling of meaning and purpose in life. You can aid others by helping them find fulfillment in their lives.

God sent his son to this Earth to be the ultimate sacrfice and servant. When you volunteer, you are walking in his footsteps and setting an example, like Jesus, by helping people that desire your help with what they need.

More Information

Who We're Looking For

We are you looking for people who desire to help others in finding Christ at university and college campuses. Having a strong foundation, and faith that is perservering and growing is essential in helping guide others. At times, our faith may grow weak, but with a strong foundation it still has power to do good. We are looking for people who have passion and a heart for God, so that they are able to guide students towards God's mission.

What You'll Receive

Being part of a group of like-minded individuals will help you grow your faith in Christ. You will also be able to receive theological seminars as well as ways to help teach others about God in such a way that is not intrusive but kind and gentle-hearted, and with an open mind and spirit. There will also be specific hourly salaries depending on which of the 4 job opportunities you decide to pursue.
