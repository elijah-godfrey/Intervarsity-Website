Pray With Us

God answers your prayers!

Why Pray?

1

Prayer helps when you feel alone

When you feel like you are all alone, remember that God is always by your side and that he hears your prayers. In the book of Matthew, Jesus tells us that if you have faith as small as a mustard seed you can do anything. You just to have faith that God will answer your prayers and he will be there for you, and will do what he knows is best.

2

Prayer helps you develop a relationship with God

Praying can help you form a relationship with God. He is always listening to your prayers, and will never leave your side. He is someone that you can trust without fail - even though you may not agree with all his decisions, He has a plan and will make sure that you find the right path.

3

God answers your prayers

Scripture shows us many times that prayers are answered and that God is always listening. Through the power of prayer, God can continue to answer your prayers.

Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God. And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus.


Philippians 4:6-7 (NIV)
